# Framework Index

## Page Goal

Give visitors a fast way to scan the operating system and jump into the right playbook based on the decision they need to make.

## Intro

These playbooks are designed for product managers who need practical tools, not abstract advice. Each one is built to improve decision quality in live product work.

## Playbooks

### Strategy

#### Problem It Solves

Teams often confuse activity with direction. They have roadmaps, themes, and stakeholder requests, but no clear logic for where to win.

#### When To Use It

- your roadmap feels busy but not directional
- teams are pursuing too many disconnected opportunities
- you need to explain why this market, segment, or wedge matters

#### What You Get

- a strategy framing model
- a market choice worksheet
- a strategy quality checklist
- an attached operator playbook for running the process live

### Prioritization

#### Problem It Solves

Prioritization often collapses into opinion, urgency theater, or a feature backlog that never gets smaller.

#### When To Use It

- the team needs a better way to compare bets

#### What You Get

- a prioritization decision stack

- an attached operator playbook for running prioritization sessions

### Metrics

#### Problem It Solves

Many teams collect more metrics than they can use. The result is reporting without learning and dashboards without decisions.

#### When To Use It

- your KPIs are broad, lagging, or disconnected from action
- the team debates data but still lacks clarity
- you need metrics that improve decisions, not just updates

#### What You Get

- a metric selection framework
- an operating metric template
- a metric review checklist
- an attached operator playbook for turning metrics into operating rhythms
